🌱 About Us: Connecting Farmers, Technology & the Future

For generations, Sri Lanka’s paddy farmers have fed our nation, but they have faced market instability, unfair pricing, and inefficiencies. Farmers struggle to get a fair price, and consumers face shortages.

This is where GrainChain comes in. We are a smart and centralized digital platform that connects farmers, distributors, and officials to make paddy management simple, efficient, and transparent.

  • ✅ Give farmers a voice – Ensuring fair prices and better opportunities.
  • ✅ Streamline the supply chain – Eliminating delays and losses.
  • ✅ Secure Sri Lanka’s food future – Making rice shortages a thing of the past.

Our mission is to create a sustainable and fair ecosystem where technology and tradition work hand in hand.

🎯 Our Mission: A Digital Revolution for Paddy & Rice

🔄 How It Works: The Smart Paddy Management System

With GrainChain, managing the paddy supply has never been easier. Here’s how it works:

  • 1️⃣ Farmers Register - Field officers collect and upload farmer details to the system.
  • 2️⃣ Paddy Collection - Paddy is gathered and stored at regional warehouses.
  • 3️⃣ Real-Time Monitoring - Officials track stock levels and predict market needs using data analytics.
  • 4️⃣ Efficient Distribution - Rice is released based on demand, preventing shortages and stabilizing prices.
  • 5️⃣ Fair Trade & Transparency - Farmers receive timely payments, and consumers benefit from a reliable rice supply.

    • It's a simple yet powerful system designed to eliminate inefficiencies and create a fair, secure, and sustainable rice market.
